Recipe Name
  • Molasses
  • Guacomole
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Cilanto
  • Lime
  • Sesame Seeds
  • 2lbs of Tofu
Instructions
  1. Chop Cilantro and slice lime on cutting board.
  2. Heat the pan for the tofu.
  3. While pan heats, chop garlic and ginger.
  4. Add molasses in a bowl with ginger and garlic and the cilantro and add tofo. Mix.
  5. Four cups of Guacomole mixed all in.
  6. Sautee over medium heat, llightly phlambe over 30 seconds, serve.
  7. Garnish with pumpkin pie spice and sesame seeds and lime.
